What RR&P is

RR&P stands for Routers, Redundancy, and Packets. Routers are devices that help direct data between networks so it is pssible for handheld devices to connec to each other. Redundancy is when your data has other ways to travel for data saftey. Packets are small pices of data that travel through the internetto other routers.
